abhibhavasi bhagavan [g]autama ' imāṃ parṣadaṃ lakṣmyā |abhibhavasi bhagavan gautama ' imāṃ
parṣadan tejasā |abhibhavasi bhagavan gautama imāṃ parṣadaṃ yaśasā |abhibhavasi bhagavan gautama
imāṃ parṣadaṃ prabhayā |abhibhavasi bhagavan gautama rūpeṇa 'suvarṇavarṇo bhagavān
gautama ātmabhāvena |aṅgiraso bhagavān gautamaḥ kāyena 'asamasamo bhagavān gautamaḥ sarvabhāvena
|āścaryādbhutadharmasamanvāgato bhagavān gautamaḥ sarvalokasya |tasya mama bhagavan
gautamaivaṃ bhavati kiṃ paśyan* bhagavān gautamo agārād anagārikāṃ pravrajitaḥ ||

atha khalu bhadrapālaḥ śreṣṭhī gṛhapatir bhagavantaṃ gāthābhir abhiṣṭauti sma ||
